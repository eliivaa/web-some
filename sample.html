CHRISTMAS

Christmas is an annual festival commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ, observed primarily on December 25 as a religious and cultural celebration among billions of people around the world. There are different hypotheses regarding the date of Jesus' birth and in the early fourth century, the church fixed the date as December 25.
